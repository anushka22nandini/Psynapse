About Us

A community built on
intellectual curiosity

PSYNAPSE is DPS Dwarka's psychology club — a space for students who are fascinated by the human mind and want to go beyond the textbook.

We host speaker sessions, case-study discussions, debates on mental health policy, and immersive workshops. Whether you're a future psychologist or simply someone who loves understanding people, this is your space.

Explore

Deep-dives into cognitive science, social psychology, and neuroscience.

Discuss

Structured debates and open forums on contemporary mental health issues.

Grow

Workshops, seminars, and mentorship from faculty and professionals.
